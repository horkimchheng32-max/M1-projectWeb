Explore the World with Confidence

I flew into Geneva in mid-September and the weather was great...nice and breezy, no rain. Switzerland's clean, one of the cleanest countries I've visited. If you're a tourist and staying in a hotel, ask the concierge for a travel pass. With the pass, you get to travel free on the metro and bus lines (within Geneva). It's a nice perk, especially in a country where the currency isn't cheap.I stayed in Geneva four nights, and took day trips to Veney, Montreux, and Lausanne; each of these cities are small enough to explore in a day.
